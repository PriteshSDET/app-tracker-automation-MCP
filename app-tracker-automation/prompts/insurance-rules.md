# Insurance Domain Rules Prompt

## Purpose
Define insurance-specific testing rules and business logic.

## Insurance Domain Knowledge
- Policy types (Life, Health, Auto, Home)
- Coverage rules and limits
- Premium calculations
- Claim processing workflows
- Underwriting guidelines
- Regulatory compliance requirements

## Testing Focus Areas
1. **Policy Management**
   - Policy creation and modification
   - Coverage validation
   - Premium calculations
   - Policy renewals and cancellations

2. **Claims Processing**
   - Claim submission
   - Document validation
   - Approval workflows
   - Payment processing

3. **Customer Management**
   - Customer registration
   - Profile management
   - Communication preferences
   - Account security

## Business Rules Validation
- Premium calculation accuracy
- Coverage limit enforcement
- Regulatory compliance checks
- Data privacy requirements
- Audit trail maintenance
